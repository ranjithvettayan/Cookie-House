Our Story

Our story began with a simple love for baking and a desire to share our passion for exceptional cookies with the world. Founded by Ranjith in 2023, Cookie's House started as a humble kitchen venture, driven by a commitment to quality and a dedication to creating memorable flavors.

Through unwavering dedication and a growing community of cookie enthusiasts, our small home-based operation has evolved into a beloved cookie brand. Every step of our journey has been guided by our core values: integrity, creativity, and a genuine love for baking.
At Cookie's House, we believe that great cookies start with great ingredients. That's why we carefully select premium components, ensuring that each cookie is a masterpiece of taste and texture. Our recipes are a blend of tradition and innovation, allowing us to offer classic favorites alongside exciting new flavors.

We take pride in not only delivering exceptional cookies but also in fostering a connection with our customers. Your satisfaction and happiness are at the heart of everything we do. Thank you for being a part of our story, and we look forward to continuing to delight you with our delicious creations.
